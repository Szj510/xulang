叙廊隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 4 日

1. 本地优先

叙廊是一款本地照片叙事和微型展览创作应用。应用不申请网络权限，不会上传你的照片、音乐、模板、录屏视频或展览数据。

2. 本地数据

你导入或引用的图片、选择的音乐、创建的展览、导出的模板和生成的视频仅保存在本机或你主动选择的位置。卸载应用会删除应用私有目录中的展览数据和缓存文件。

3. 文件夹授权

当你授权某个本地文件夹时，叙廊只会扫描该文件夹中可能的模板文件和常见音频文件，用于模板导入和音乐选择。叙廊不会把这些文件发送到任何服务器。

4. 录屏权限

叙廊仅在你主动开始录屏播放时请求系统录屏权限。录屏生成的 MP4 文件保存在本机。你可以自行预览、删除或分享这些文件。

5. 分享到第三方应用

当你选择分享模板或视频时，文件会交给系统分享面板和你选择的第三方应用处理。第三方应用可能会压缩视频、生成副本或执行自己的隐私政策；这些行为不由叙廊控制。

6. 儿童与账号

叙廊不提供账号系统，不收集身份信息，也不面向儿童定向收集数据。

7. 联系方式

如需反馈隐私或安全问题，请通过邮箱联系开发者：suzijie510@gmail.com

Xulang Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

1. Local-first design

Xulang is a local photo storytelling and micro-gallery creation app. The app does not request network permission and does not upload your photos, music, templates, screen recordings, or exhibition data.

2. Local data

Photos you import or reference, music you choose, exhibitions you create, templates you export, and videos you generate are stored only on your device or in locations you explicitly choose. Uninstalling the app removes exhibition data and cache files stored in the app-private directory.

3. Folder access

When you grant access to a local folder, Xulang only scans that folder for possible template files and common audio files for template import and music selection. Xulang does not send these files to any server.

4. Screen recording permission

Xulang requests the system screen recording permission only when you actively start recording playback. Generated MP4 files are saved on your device. You can preview, delete, or share those files yourself.

5. Sharing to third-party apps

When you choose to share templates or videos, files are handed to the system share sheet and the third-party app you select. Third-party apps may compress videos, create copies, or apply their own privacy policies; these actions are not controlled by Xulang.

6. Children and accounts

Xulang does not provide an account system, does not collect identity information, and does not knowingly collect data directed at children.

7. Contact

For privacy or security questions, contact the developer by email: suzijie510@gmail.com
