# Code of Conduct / 社区行为准则

## Our pledge / 我们的承诺

We pledge to make participation in Xulang a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

我们承诺为所有参与者提供免受骚扰的协作环境，不因年龄、身体状况、残障、族裔、性别特征、性别认同与表达、经验、教育、经济状况、国籍、外貌、种族、阶层、肤色、宗教或性取向而区别对待。

## Expected behavior / 期望行为

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Give and accept feedback about the work, not the person.
- Respect privacy and obtain permission before publishing another person's content.
- Acknowledge mistakes and repair harm where possible.

- 保持尊重、耐心和建设性。
- 针对作品本身提出或接受反馈，不进行人身攻击。
- 尊重隐私，公开他人内容前先获得许可。
- 承认错误，并尽力修复造成的影响。

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, sexualized language or imagery, insults, threats, stalking, publishing private information, deliberate disruption, or conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

不可接受的行为包括骚扰、色情化语言或图像、侮辱、威胁、跟踪、公开私人信息、蓄意破坏协作，以及其他在专业环境中明显不当的行为。

## Enforcement / 执行

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this policy, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

维护者可以删除、编辑或拒绝违反本准则的评论、提交、代码、Issue 或其他贡献，并可暂时或永久限制相关人员参与。

Report conduct incidents privately to `3288085966@qq.com`. The maintainer will respect the reporter's privacy and review the situation fairly. Do not use public issues for enforcement reports.

行为准则事件请私下发送至 `3288085966@qq.com`。维护者会保护报告者隐私并公平处理，请勿使用公开 Issue 举报。
